Monster Mash

A spooky scary card matching game!

A card matching game featuring friendly monsters. Fun for everyone!

Hover on the squares to get more info!

  • M

    The Monsters

    The monsters featured on the cards are from the Universal Classic Monsters collectionand from modern times animated films. The monster icons are by iconcheese from the Noun Project.

  • A

    About

    This game was inspired by my love of monsters, vintage cinema and of course, the ultimate banger, Monster Mash.

  • S

    Start!

    Are you ready to start mashing monsters!? Hit the MASH button. Monsters will apear and the mash will begin!

  • H

    How to play

    Click on the cards to make pairs. Once all matches have been found, you win! Try again to beat your move count.
    This game is currently not keyboard accessible.
